Bhumi Bambu

Playing at the waterfalls in Baturaden, Purwokerto, was an unforgettable experience. Upon arriving at the location for the first time, the sensation of the cool water touching the skin was incredibly refreshing. It felt like a reconnection with nature as the sound of the flowing water and the surrounding lush trees created a peaceful ambiance. Then, while swimming in the pool formed beneath the waterfall, I felt like I was in a hidden paradise, far away from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

Furthermore, the adventures around the waterfall offered a lot of excitement. My friends and I explored the steep cliffs and discovered hidden spots that provided spectacular views. Free-falling from small rocks and experiencing that adrenaline rush was another unforgettable experience. Baturaden is the perfect place to escape from the daily routine and savor the beauty of nature while enjoying thrilling adventures under the sun.
